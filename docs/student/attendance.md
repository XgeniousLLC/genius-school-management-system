# My Attendance

## Viewing Your Attendance Record

Go to **My Attendance** in the sidebar. You will see your attendance for the **last 6 months**.

---

## Summary Cards

At the top, summary cards show:

- **Total School Days** — how many days school was in session
- **Days Present** — how many days you attended
- **Days Absent** — how many days you were marked absent
- **Attendance %** — your overall attendance percentage

---

## Monthly Breakdown

Below the summary, each month is shown as a calendar. Each day is marked with a coloured dot:

| Colour | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Green | Present |
| Red | Absent |
| Yellow / Amber | Late |
| Grey | No school / Holiday |

---

## What to Do If Attendance Is Wrong

If you believe your attendance has been recorded incorrectly, inform your class teacher or school admin. They can correct it from the admin panel.

> Attendance is marked by your teacher each day. You cannot edit it yourself.

# My Homework

## Viewing Assignments

Go to **My Homework** in the sidebar. Assignments are divided into two sections:

### Overdue
Homework whose deadline has already passed. These are shown at the top so you can see what you have missed.

### Pending
Homework that is still due — the deadline has not passed yet. These are sorted by due date, with the most urgent first.

---

## Reading an Assignment

Each homework card shows:

- **Subject** — which subject the assignment is for
- **Title** — the name of the assignment
- **Description** — instructions from your teacher
- **Due Date** — when it must be submitted
- **Assigned By** — which teacher set it

---

## Submitting Homework

Submission is done in class or as instructed by your teacher. The portal shows you what has been assigned but submission is handled offline (or as your school specifies).

---

## No Homework Showing?

If no homework appears, either:
- Your teachers have not assigned any yet, or
- Homework has been assigned to a different section

Contact your teacher if you believe an assignment is missing.

# My Timetable

## Viewing Your Weekly Schedule

Go to **My Timetable** in the sidebar. You will see a full weekly grid showing all your classes from Monday to Friday (or your school's working days).

Each cell in the grid shows:
- Subject name
- Teacher name
- Room / location

**Today's column is highlighted** so you can quickly see what classes you have right now.

---

## Reading the Timetable

- **Rows** represent time slots (periods) during the day.
- **Columns** represent days of the week.
- Empty cells mean you have a free period or break at that time.

---

## Changes to the Timetable

If your timetable changes (a teacher is absent, a class is rescheduled), your school admin will update it. Simply refresh the page to see the latest version.

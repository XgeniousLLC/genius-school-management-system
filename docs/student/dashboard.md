# Student Dashboard

The dashboard gives you a quick snapshot of your school day and upcoming events.

---

## Today's Schedule

The left panel shows your classes for **today**, in order of time. Each card shows:

- Subject name
- Room / location
- Start and end time
- Teacher name

If there are no classes today (holiday or weekend), the panel will show a message accordingly.

---

## Upcoming Exams

On the right you will see a list of exams scheduled in the near future:

- Exam name and subject
- Date
- How many days away it is

Use this to plan your study schedule.

---

## Recent Announcements

The bottom of the dashboard shows the latest announcements from your school or class. **Pinned** announcements (highlighted in amber) are important notices that the school wants everyone to see.

Click any announcement to read the full message.

---

## Quick Summary Cards

At the top of the dashboard you may see summary cards showing:

- Attendance percentage
- Pending homework count
- Outstanding fee balance

These give you a fast overview without needing to visit each section.

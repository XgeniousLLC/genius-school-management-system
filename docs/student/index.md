# Student Guide

Welcome to the student portal. Once your school admin links your account, you can log in and access everything related to your academic life — timetable, attendance, exam results, homework, and fees.

---

## Your Sidebar Menu

| Section | What You Can See |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Today's schedule, upcoming exams, recent announcements |
| **My Timetable** | Your weekly class schedule |
| **My Attendance** | Your attendance record for the last 6 months |
| **My Results** | Your exam marks and grades |
| **My Homework** | Assignments from your teachers |
| **My Fees** | Your fee payments and outstanding balance |
| **Announcements** | School-wide and class announcements |

---

## Logging In

1. Open the school portal URL provided by your school.
2. Enter the **email address** and **password** given to you.
3. You will be taken directly to your student dashboard.

> If you see **"Account not linked"**, contact your school admin — your portal account needs to be linked to your student record.

---

## Getting Help

If something looks wrong (wrong class, missing results, incorrect fees), speak to your school admin or class teacher. They can update your records from the admin panel.

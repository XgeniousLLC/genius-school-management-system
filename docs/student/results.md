# My Results

## Viewing Your Exam Results

Go to **My Results** in the sidebar. Your results are grouped by exam — for example, "First Term Exam" or "Mid-Year Exam".

---

## Reading Your Results

Each exam section shows a table with:

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Subject | The subject you were examined in |
| Full Marks | Maximum possible marks |
| Marks Obtained | Your score |
| Grade | Your letter grade (A, B, C, etc.) |
| Remarks | Pass / Fail or teacher comment |

---

## Grade Colours

Grades are colour-coded to make them easy to read at a glance:

- **Green** — Excellent / High grade
- **Blue** — Good / Above average
- **Yellow** — Average / Satisfactory
- **Red** — Below passing / Fail

---

## Results Not Showing?

Results are published by your school admin after the exam is complete and marks are entered. If you cannot see results for a recent exam, they may not have been published yet. Check back later or ask your teacher.

# Timetable

Go to **Academic → Timetable** to build and manage weekly class schedules.

---

## Creating a Timetable Slot

1. Go to **Academic → Timetable**.
2. Select the **Class** and **Section** you want to schedule.
3. Click **Add Slot**.
4. Fill in:
   - **Day** (Monday through Saturday/Sunday)
   - **Start Time** and **End Time**
   - **Subject**
   - **Room** (optional, e.g. Room 101, Lab A)
5. Click **Save**.

Repeat for each period of the week.

---

## Editing or Deleting a Slot

- Click the **Edit** icon on any slot to change the time, subject, or room.
- Click the **Delete** icon to remove a slot.

---

## Viewing the Full Timetable

The timetable is displayed as a grid showing each day of the week with time slots. You can switch between classes using the class/section selector at the top.

---

## How Students See Their Timetable

Students can view their own timetable by going to **My Timetable** in the Student Portal. Today's schedule is also highlighted on their dashboard.

---

## Tips

- Build the timetable after you have added all subjects and assigned teachers.
- Make sure the start and end times of slots do not overlap for the same class.

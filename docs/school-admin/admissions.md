# Admissions & Visitors

---

## Admission Inquiries

Track every inquiry from prospective students and their families.

### Receiving a New Inquiry

1. Go to **Admissions → Inquiries**.
2. Click **Add Inquiry**.
3. Fill in:
   - Student name, date of birth
   - Parent/guardian name and contact
   - Class applying for
   - Source (Walk-in, Phone, Website, Referral)
   - Any notes
4. Click **Save**.

The inquiry starts with a status of **New**.

### Following Up on an Inquiry

1. Open any inquiry from the list.
2. Click **Add Follow-up**.
3. Enter your notes about the conversation or visit.
4. Click **Save Follow-up**.

All follow-up notes are timestamped and stored under the inquiry.

### Inquiry Status Workflow

Move an inquiry through these stages as it progresses:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **New** | Just received, not yet contacted |
| **Contacted** | You have reached out to the family |
| **Scheduled** | A visit or meeting has been arranged |
| **Admitted** | Student has been enrolled |
| **Rejected** | Inquiry was not converted |

To change the status, open the inquiry and select the new status from the dropdown.


Submitted forms appear automatically in your Inquiries list.

## Visitor Log

Keep a record of all visitors to the school.

### Logging a Visitor Check-In

1. Go to **Admissions → Visitors**.
2. Click **Check In Visitor**.
3. Enter:
   - Visitor name
   - Phone number
   - Purpose of visit
   - Person they are meeting
4. Click **Check In** — the arrival time is recorded automatically.

### Checking Out a Visitor

1. Find the visitor in the list.
2. Click **Check Out** — the departure time is recorded automatically.

You can see all current visitors (those checked in but not yet out) at the top of the list.

# Transport

Go to **Facilities → Transport** to manage school vehicles and routes.

---

## Adding a Vehicle

1. Go to **Transport → Vehicles**.
2. Click **Add Vehicle**.
3. Enter the vehicle number, type (bus, van), driver name, capacity, and contact number.
4. Click **Save**.

---

## Adding a Route

1. Go to **Transport → Routes**.
2. Click **Add Route**.
3. Enter the route name and add stops with their pick-up times.
4. Assign a vehicle to the route.
5. Click **Save**.

---

## Assigning a Student to a Route

1. Open the student's profile or edit their record.
2. In the **Transport** section, select the route they use.
3. Click **Save**.

Students assigned to a route are visible in the route details page.

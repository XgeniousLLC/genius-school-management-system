# School Admin Guide

As a School Admin, you have full control over your school. This guide covers everything you need to manage your school from day one.

## Where to Begin

If this is a new school setup, follow these steps in order:

1. [Configure your school settings](/school-admin/setup) — name, logo, timezone, academic year
2. [Add classes and sections](/school-admin/setup#classes-and-sections)
3. [Add subjects](/school-admin/setup#subjects)
4. [Add staff members](/school-admin/staff)
5. [Enrol students](/school-admin/students)
6. [Build the timetable](/school-admin/timetable)
7. [Set up fee structures](/school-admin/fees)

## Your Dashboard

After logging in, you land on the **Reports Dashboard** which shows:

- Total students and staff
- Today's attendance percentage
- This month's fee collection
- Outstanding fees
- A fee collection chart for the last 6 months
- Attendance trends for the last 7 days
- Recent activity log

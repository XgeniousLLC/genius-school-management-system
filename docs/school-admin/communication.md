# Communication

---

## Announcements

Post announcements that are visible to students, parents, teachers, or everyone.

### Creating an Announcement

1. Go to **Communication → Announcements**.
2. Click **Add Announcement**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Title**
   - **Body** — the full announcement text
   - **Audience** — choose who sees it:
     - All (everyone in the school)
     - Students only
     - Parents only
     - Staff only
     - A specific class
   - **Pin this announcement** — pinned announcements appear at the top with an amber highlight
   - **Publish Date**
4. Click **Save**.

### Editing or Deleting an Announcement

Find the announcement in the list and click **Edit** or **Delete** from the Actions column.

---

## Messages

Send and receive private messages between staff members.

1. Go to **Communication → Messages**.
2. Click **Compose**.
3. Select the recipient, enter a subject and message body.
4. Click **Send**.

Your inbox shows all received messages. Click any message to read and reply.

---

## SMS / Email Blast

Send a bulk message to all students, all parents, or a specific class.

1. Go to **Communication → SMS/Email Blast**.
2. Choose the channel — **SMS** or **Email**.
3. Select your audience (all students, all parents, a specific class, etc.).
4. Write your message.
5. Click **Send** — the message is queued and delivered in the background.

> SMS requires your school's SMS gateway to be configured in Settings.

---

## Notifications

The bell icon in the top-right corner shows system notifications such as:

- New leave requests awaiting approval
- Fee payment confirmations
- New admission inquiries
- System alerts

Click the bell to see all notifications and mark them as read.

# Examinations & Results

Go to **Academic → Examinations** to create exams, enter marks, and manage student results.

---

## Creating an Exam

1. Go to **Academic → Examinations**.
2. Click **Add Exam**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Exam Name** (e.g. Mid-Term Exam 2025)
   - **Type** — Midterm, Final, Quiz, or Assignment
   - **Class** this exam applies to
   - **Start Date** and **End Date**
4. Click **Save Exam**.

---

## Entering Marks

1. Open the exam from the list.
2. Click **Enter Marks**.
3. Select the **Subject**.
4. For each student enter:
   - **Marks Obtained** (e.g. 78)
   - **Total Marks** (e.g. 100)
   - Tick **Absent** if the student did not sit the exam
5. The **Grade** is calculated automatically based on your grade scale.
6. Click **Save Marks**.

Repeat for each subject in the exam.

---

## Grade Scale

The system calculates grades automatically:

| Percentage | Grade |
|---|---|
| 80% and above | A+ |
| 70% – 79% | A |
| 60% – 69% | B |
| 50% – 59% | C |
| 40% – 49% | D |
| Below 40% | F |

> Your administrator may have configured a custom grade scale for your school.

---

## Viewing Results

1. Open any exam.
2. Click **View Results** to see a summary table of all students with their marks and grades per subject.

Students can view their own results in the **Student Portal** under **My Results**.

---

## Exam Status

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Upcoming** | Exam is scheduled but has not started |
| **Ongoing** | Exam is currently in progress |
| **Completed** | Exam is finished; marks can be entered |
| **Results Published** | Results are visible to students and parents |

# Attendance

## Viewing Your Child's Attendance

Go to **Attendance** in the sidebar. You will see your child's attendance record for the **last 6 months**.

---

## Summary

At the top, summary cards show:

| Card | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Total School Days** | How many days school was open |
| **Days Present** | How many days your child attended |
| **Days Absent** | How many days they were absent |
| **Attendance %** | Overall attendance rate |

---

## Monthly Calendar View

Each month is shown as a calendar. Each day is colour-coded:

| Colour | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Green | Present |
| Red | Absent |
| Yellow / Amber | Late |
| Grey | No school / Holiday |

---

## If the Record Is Wrong

Attendance is recorded by the class teacher each day. If you believe a day has been marked incorrectly, contact the school and ask the admin or teacher to review it.

> You cannot edit attendance yourself from the parent portal.

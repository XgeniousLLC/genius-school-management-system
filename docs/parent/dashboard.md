# Parent Dashboard

The dashboard gives you a quick overview of your child's current school status.

---

## Summary Cards

At the top you will find key numbers at a glance:

| Card | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Attendance %** | Your child's attendance rate this term |
| **Pending Fees** | Outstanding fee balance |
| **Recent Results** | Latest exam result summary |

---

## Recent Announcements

The dashboard shows the latest announcements from the school. **Pinned announcements** (highlighted in amber) are important notices the school wants all parents to see.

---

## Navigating to Details

Use the sidebar links to go deeper into any area:

- **Attendance** — see the full day-by-day record
- **Results** — view all exam results
- **Fee Status** — check payment history
- **Announcements** — read all announcements

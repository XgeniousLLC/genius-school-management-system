# Fee Status

## Checking Your Child's Fee Payments

Go to **Fee Status** in the sidebar. You will see a summary and full payment history for your child.

---

## Summary Cards

| Card | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Total Charged** | Total fees assigned across all months |
| **Total Paid** | Total amount paid so far |
| **Outstanding Balance** | Amount still owed |

A balance of **zero** means all fees are paid up to date.

---

## Payment History Table

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Month / Period | The month the payment covers |
| Amount Due | Fee amount for that period |
| Amount Paid | How much was paid |
| Status | Paid, Partial, or Unpaid |
| Date | Date the payment was recorded |

---

## Partial Payments

If less than the full amount was paid for a month, the status shows **Partial**. The remaining amount is added to your outstanding balance.

---

## Fee Reminders

If there is an outstanding balance, the school may send an SMS or email reminder to your registered contact. Make sure the school has your correct phone number and email address.

---

## Discrepancies

If a payment you made is not showing, contact the school accountant with your payment receipt. They will verify and update the record.

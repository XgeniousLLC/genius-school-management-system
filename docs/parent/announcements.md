# Announcements

## Viewing School Announcements

Go to **Announcements** in the sidebar to see all messages from the school.

---

## Types of Announcements

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Pinned** | Important notices highlighted in amber — read these first |
| **Regular** | General information and updates |

Pinned announcements stay at the top of the list so you never miss important information.

---

## What Announcements Cover

The school uses announcements to communicate:

- Upcoming holidays or school closures
- Exam schedules and important dates
- School events, trips, or parent-teacher meetings
- General notices to parents and students

---

## Staying Updated

Check the Announcements page regularly, especially before school events or exam periods. If the school has enabled notifications, you may also receive an alert when a new announcement is posted.

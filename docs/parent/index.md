# Parent Guide

The parent portal lets you monitor your child's school life — attendance, exam results, fee payments, and school announcements — all in one place.

---

## Your Sidebar Menu

| Section | What You Can See |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Overview of your child's attendance, results, and fees |
| **Attendance** | Your child's daily attendance record |
| **Results** | Exam marks and grades |
| **Fee Status** | Fee payments and outstanding balance |
| **Announcements** | School and class announcements |

---

## Logging In

1. Open the school portal URL provided by the school.
2. Enter the **email** and **password** given to you by the school admin.
3. You will be taken to the parent dashboard.

> If you see **"Account not linked"**, your account has not been connected to your child's record yet. Contact the school admin.

---

## Multiple Children

If you have more than one child at this school and both are linked to your account, you may see data for all of them. Contact the school admin to ensure all your children are linked.

---

## Keeping Information Updated

If your contact details (phone number, email) change, inform the school admin so they can update the records. Fee reminders and important notifications are sent to the contact details on file.

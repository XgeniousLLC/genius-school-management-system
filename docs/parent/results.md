# Results

## Viewing Your Child's Exam Results

Go to **Results** in the sidebar. Results are grouped by exam — for example "First Term Exam" or "Annual Exam".

---

## Reading the Results Table

Each exam section shows:

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Subject | The subject examined |
| Full Marks | Maximum possible marks |
| Marks Obtained | Your child's score |
| Grade | Letter grade (A, B, C, etc.) |
| Remarks | Pass / Fail or teacher comment |

---

## Grade Colours

Grades are colour-coded for quick reading:

- **Green** — Excellent
- **Blue** — Good
- **Yellow** — Average / Satisfactory
- **Red** — Below passing / Fail

---

## Results Not Published Yet

Results appear after the school admin publishes them. If a recent exam is not showing, it may not have been published yet. Check back later or contact the school.

# Finance Report

## Opening the Finance Report

Go to **Finance Report** in your sidebar. The report gives you a full financial overview for the school.

---

## Fee Collection Summary

At the top you will see summary cards showing:

- **Total Collected** — all payments received so far
- **Total Outstanding** — fees still owed across all students
- **This Month** — fees collected in the current month

---

## Collection by Class

A table breaks down fee collection per class — how much was expected, how much was collected, and how much is outstanding. Use this to identify which classes have the most unpaid fees.

---

## Recent Payments

A list of the most recent fee payments across all students, showing the student name, amount, and date.

---

## Payroll Summary

The report also shows the current month's payroll — total salary expenditure and a list of processed staff salaries.

---

## Exporting

Use the **Export** or **Print** button (if available) to save or print the report for records or auditing purposes.

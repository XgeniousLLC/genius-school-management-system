# Navigating the System

## The Sidebar

The sidebar on the left side of the screen is your main navigation menu. It is organised into groups such as **Academic**, **Finance**, **Communication**, and **Reports**.

- Only the sections you have permission to access are shown.
- Click the **◀ arrow** at the top of the sidebar to collapse it to icons only — useful on smaller screens.
- Click **▶** to expand it again.

## The Topbar

The topbar at the top of every page shows:

- The **current page title** on the left.
- Your **profile name and role** on the right — click it to log out or change your password.
- A **dark/light mode** toggle (moon/sun icon).

## Breadcrumbs

On most pages you will see breadcrumbs below the topbar (e.g. **Students › Add Student**) so you always know where you are.

## Tables and Lists

Most pages that show lists of records include:

- A **search box** to filter records by name, number, or other fields.
- **Column headers** you can click to sort.
- **Pagination** at the bottom to move between pages.
- An **Actions** column with buttons to view, edit, or delete each record.

## Forms

When adding or editing a record:

1. Fill in all required fields (marked with **\***).
2. Click **Save** or **Submit** to save your changes.
3. Click **Cancel** to discard changes and go back.

A green success banner appears at the top of the page when changes are saved successfully.

## Tips

- Use the **browser back button** or the breadcrumbs to go back — avoid closing the tab.
- Most delete actions ask for confirmation before anything is removed.
- If you see a **red error banner**, read the message — it usually tells you exactly what needs to be corrected.

# Timetable & Schedule

Go to **Academic → Timetable** to view the weekly timetable for your classes.

The timetable shows each day of the week with the time, subject, and room for each class period. Use the **Class** and **Section** dropdowns to switch between your different classes.

> Only the school admin or principal can create or edit timetable slots. If you notice an error, contact your admin.

# Taking Attendance

1. Go to **Academic → Attendance**.
2. Select your **Class**, **Section**, and today's **Date**.
3. Click **Load Students**.
4. Mark each student as **Present**, **Absent**, **Late**, or **Excused**.
5. Add a remark for any student if needed.
6. Click **Submit Attendance**.

> Do this every day at the start of class. Once submitted, attendance is saved and visible to the school admin and in reports.

## What Happens After You Submit

- The school admin can see attendance in the Reports Dashboard.
- Parents receive an SMS notification if their child is marked absent (if enabled by admin).
- Students can see their own attendance record in the Student Portal.

## Correcting a Mistake

If you made an error, load the same class/section/date again. The previously saved statuses will be shown — change what needs correcting and click **Update Attendance**.

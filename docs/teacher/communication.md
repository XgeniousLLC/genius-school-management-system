# Communication

---

## Posting an Announcement

1. Go to **Communication → Announcements**.
2. Click **Add Announcement**.
3. Write your title and message.
4. Set the **Audience** — you can target all students, a specific class, or all parents.
5. Optionally **pin** it so it appears at the top for everyone.
6. Click **Save**.

---

## Sending a Message

1. Go to **Communication → Messages**.
2. Click **Compose**.
3. Select the recipient (another staff member).
4. Write your message and click **Send**.

---

## Notifications

The **bell icon** in the top-right corner shows notifications relevant to you, such as new messages or upcoming exam reminders. Click it to view and dismiss notifications.
